## Projects

### [Wind Farm Digital Twin — Kelmarsh](https://github.com/miguelRepo/kelmarsh-digital-twin)
LSTM autoencoder for turbine anomaly detection trained on Kelmarsh SCADA data,
combined with FLORIS wake modeling for yaw optimization. Identifies bearing
faults and power anomalies across 6 turbines, with up to 5.6% AEP gain from
wake steering.  
`Python` `PyTorch` `FLORIS` `Streamlit` `Docker`

### [NACA Airfoil Surrogate Model](https://github.com/miguelRepo/naca-surrogate)
Random Forest surrogate model trained on 20,000+ XFoil aerodynamic simulations.
Predicts lift and drag coefficients for NACA 4-digit airfoils instantly from
geometry and flight conditions.  
**[Live demo →](https://naca-airfoil-surrogate.streamlit.app)**  
`Python` `XFoil` `scikit-learn` `Streamlit`

### [US Aviation Accident Analysis (2008–2025)](https://github.com/miguelRepo/aircraft-accidents-analysis)
Exploratory analysis of 18,498 NTSB aviation accident records. Covers accident
trends, phase-of-flight risk, human factors, and IMC vs VMC fatality rates.  
`Python` `Pandas` `Matplotlib` `SQLite`
